How we met
Come ci siamo conosciuti

Lhen, a dedicated teacher, spent her weekends diving into her passion for marine life. One sunny Saturday, she joined a scuba diving trip to unwind from the workweek.

On that same trip was Stef, a man with a deep love for the ocean. As fate would have it, their paths crossed during a dive. Stef noticed Lhen's enthusiasm and struck up a conversation. They instantly connected over their shared passion for the sea and their desire to explore its wonders.

Throughout the day, they swam through vibrant coral reefs and marveled at the underwater beauty, their bond growing with each dive. By the end of the trip, it was clear that this was the beginning of something special.

Today, we celebrate Lhen and Stef's love story, which started beneath the waves and has flourished ever since. Welcome to their wedding, a beautiful union of two souls brought together by a shared love for the ocean.

How to get there?
Come arrivare?

Air

The closest airport is Pisa Galileo Galilei but if you find a better deal Florence or Milan could also be an option.
Renting a car from there will offer you flexibility, otherwise you can reach the town of Lucca and then the town of Borgo a Mozzano via bus.

As soon we get confirmation we might find a shared solution based on your travel date.
Please send us any info if required in the form below.


L'aeroporto piú vicino é Pisa Galileo Galilei ma se trovate un'offerta migliore, Firenze o Milano puó essere un opzione.
Noleggiare una macchina offre sicuramente la comoditá di raggiungere il posto autonomamente ma se ció non é possibile bisogna prima raggiungere Lucca e poi Borgo a Mozzano via bus.

Dopo aver ricevuto le dovute conferme possiamo pensare insieme una soluzione di trasporto condivisa.
Non dimenticare di indicarlo nella form qui sotto.

Car

You can certainly find route direction using the map link below or your navigation system.
If you are interested on a panoramic route while still being fast, you can exit the highway in Viareggio. If you want to take slow then the Appennino Tosco-Emiliano is for you.
Don't forget to enjoy nature but don't be late as well!


Sicuramente puoi trovare la direzione usando la mappa qui sotto o il tuo navigatore di fiducia.
Se sei interessato a una via panoramica, puoi uscire dall'autostrada A15 a Viareggio e usare le strade locali. Se invece vuoi prenderla comodo, allora esplorare l'Appenino Tosco-Emiliano é per te!
Non dimenticare di apprezzare la natura, ma cerca ugualmente di non arrivare in ritardo!
